The performance ratio is calculated as follows

PRmeasured = E × Gstc Pp × Gpoa
PRmeasured Performance ratio (measured)
E Electricity generation [kWh] measured at Owner side of the Plant Step-up Substation located on Site
Gstc Installed capacity [kWp] as determined according to the provisions of the EPC Contract
Pp Total global solar irradiation sum on the plane of array without possible downtimes [kWh/m2] measured during the test periods
Gpoa Global solar irradiance under STC = 1000 W/m2
